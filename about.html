Morales, Trisha Mia G.


I am a 2nd-year Bachelor of Science in Information Technology student at the Technological University of the Philippines — Taguig Campus. My academic journey has led me into the intersection of technology and creative media through the subject Multimedia and Authoring.

Through this course, I have explored rotoscoping animation, 3D modeling in Blender, video editing, and web design. Each project in this portfolio represents a step forward in understanding how technology can be used as a medium for visual storytelling.

Outside of multimedia, I write code in various languages and enjoy problem-solving through programming. I believe that the union of technical skill and creative thinking produces the most meaningful digital work.
